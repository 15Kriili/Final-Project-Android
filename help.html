 Instructions- Control the spaceship with the wasd keys if you are playing on a computer, or if you are playing on a phone, use the buttons to control the spaceship. Your object is to collect as many yellow stars as possible (they give bonus points) but the red star makes you lose points. Getting to Earth is your main goal, after landing on Earth you will be prompted for a name, and then your name and score will be entered into the leaderboards after you press the button!
